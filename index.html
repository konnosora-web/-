Newfoundland & Labrador

🏙️ Notable Cities
  • St. John's
    The capital city known for colorful houses, historic streets, and ocean views.
  • Corner Brook
    A beautiful city surrounded by forests, mountains, and hiking trails.
  • Happy Valley–Goose Bay
    A major Labrador community famous for wildlife and outdoor adventures.

🏄 Fun Things to Do
  • Iceberg Watching
    See giant icebergs that drift south from Greenland every spring.
  • Whale Watching
    Watch humpback whales leap from the water along the rugged coastline.
  • Coastal Hiking
    Explore scenic trails with cliffs, wildflowers, and ocean views.

⛰️ Cool Geo Features
  • Gros Morne National Park
    A UNESCO World Heritage Site with mountains, fjords, and unique landscapes.
  • Western Brook Pond
    A beautiful freshwater fjord surrounded by cliffs and waterfalls.
  • The Tablelands
    A rare place where Earth's mantle rocks can be seen on the surface.

🍟 Food to Try
  • Fish & Chips
    Fresh Atlantic cod covered with crispy batter. A famous local seafood meal.
  • Jiggs Dinner
    A traditional dish with salt beef, vegetables, and peas pudding.
  • Toutons
    Fried bread dough usually enjoyed with butter and sweet molasses.

🗓️ Best Time to Visit
  • May – June
    The best season to see both whales and icebergs together.
  • July – August
    Warm weather is perfect for hiking, boat tours, and festivals.
  • September – October
    Enjoy colorful autumn scenery with fewer visitors.
🐶 Famous Dogs
  • Labrador Retriever
    Friendly, smart, and loyal. One of the world's most popular dogs.
  • Newfoundland Dog
    A giant gentle dog famous for swimming and water rescue.
  • Rescue Heritage
    These dogs helped fishermen and saved people near the ocean.
