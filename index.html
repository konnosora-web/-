Newfoundland & Labrador

🏙️ Famous Cities
  • St. John's
    This is the capital city. It has many colorful houses. You can see the sea.
  • Corner Brook
    This city has many trees and mountains. You can walk in the mountains.
  • Happy Valley–Goose Bay
    This is a big city in Labrador. You can see many animals here.
🏄 Fun Things
  • Icebergs
    In spring, very big pieces of ice come from the north. You can see them near the sea!
  • Whales
    You can watch big whales jump out of the water. It is very exciting!
  • Walking
    You can walk near the sea. The views are very beautiful.
⛰️ Great Places
  • Gros Morne Park
    This park is very famous in the world. It has big mountains and lakes.
  • Western Brook Pond
    This is a long, thin lake. It has very tall rocks around it.
  • The Tablelands
    This is a very special place. The rocks here are from deep in the Earth.
🍟 Good Food
  • Fish & Chips
    This is fish and potato. It is very popular here. It is delicious!
  • Jiggs Dinner
    This is an old dish. It has meat and vegetables.
  • Toutons
    This is fried bread. You eat it with butter. It is sweet and good!
🗓️ Good Time to Visit
  • May – June
    This is the best time! You can see whales and big ice.
  • July – August
    It is warm. Good for walking and boat tours.
  • September – October
    The trees are red and yellow. It is very beautiful!
🐶 Famous Dogs
  • Labrador Retriever
    This dog is kind and smart. It is very popular all over the world.
  • Newfoundland Dog
    This dog is very big. It is good at swimming in the water.
  • Helper Dogs
    These dogs helped fishermen. They saved people in the sea.
